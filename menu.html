KAI

Haukai Restaurant specialises in Maori kai and is based in the Bay of Islands. It was started by a couple who are keen on creating a business that would appeal to locals and tourists visiting the area. The owners invite customers to experience Te Ao Maori with the use of karakia for blessing food. Haukai offers a wide range of authentic Maori and Pakeha kai options and drinks.

Our menus change with the seasons. The innovative dishes reflect what is currently in season through locally sourced produce from trusted vendors across the Aotearoa! Haukai pairs it's amazing cuisine with the best wine that is locally produced from some of the finest vineyards around the country, and offers a wide range of largers and ales crafted from New Zealand's top breweries.

Regardless of taste, there is something on offer for everyone.
