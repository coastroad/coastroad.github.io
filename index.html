Welcome to Haukai

Haukai Restaurant specialises in Maori kai and is based in the Bay of Islands. It was started by a couple who are keen on creating a business that would appeal to locals and tourists visiting the area. The owners invite customers to experience Te Ao Maori with the use of karakia for blessing food. Haukai offers a wide range of authentic Maori and Pakeha kai options and drinks.
